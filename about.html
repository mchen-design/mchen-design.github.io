Designer, Student, Athlete, Advocate.

I am a senior studying Cognitive Science at UC Berkeley,
where I am also the Fall 2020 Chief Operations Officer for
the Invention Corps (ICB).

🙌 Why Design?

I followed an almost-full-time athlete’s schedule in high
school; weekdays and weekends were taken up by training,
and holidays were spent on the road to competitions in
other countries.

Looking back, I faced a certain dimension of discipline and
adversity as an athlete, and I think that’s why I naturally lean
toward areas where I can stand tall (for myself and others)
when times are tough—in short, finding the silver lining in a
challenge. To me, the impact that human-centered design
and its subdivisions have on the world is that silver lining.

I have found so much joy in using my skills to strengthen
and empower the creative load of the teams I am on and,
most importantly, the lives of the individuals I design for. 💭
